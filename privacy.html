Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-23

Who we are

This privacy policy applies to Concrete Contractors Kenosha ("we," "us," "our"), a concrete contracting business operating in Kenosha County, Wisconsin. Reachable at (262) 555-0148.

Information we collect

We only collect the information you give us directly through:

  • Quote request forms: name, phone number, email (optional), project address, service interest, and any project details you provide.
  • Direct calls or emails: whatever contact info you share when you reach out.
  • Analytics: we use Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors find and use this site, page views, referrer, approximate location (city level), device type. GA4 is configured with IP anonymization. No personal information is sent to GA4.

How we use it

We use your information only to:

  • Respond to your quote request and follow up about your project
  • Send you a written estimate
  • Schedule and coordinate on-site work if you hire us
  • Send a thank-you and request a review after project completion

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with marketing companies, lead-resellers, or any third party. We do not use your data for retargeting ads.

Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies: Google Analytics sets _ga and _ga_* cookies to recognize repeat visitors anonymously. You can disable cookies in your browser without losing site functionality.

Data retention

We retain quote-request information for as long as needed to complete the project and meet our legal/accounting requirements (typically 7 years in Wisconsin for service-business records). Analytics data is retained per Google's defaults (14 months).

Your rights

You can request a copy of the information we hold about you, ask us to delete it, or opt out of follow-up communication at any time. Call (262) 555-0148.

Security

Form submissions are transmitted via HTTPS. Our hosting provider (Cloudflare Pages) maintains industry-standard security practices. We do not store payment information on our website, invoicing is handled through QuickBooks or check.

Changes to this policy

If we change this policy we will update the "Last updated" date at the top. Material changes will be noted on the homepage for at least 30 days.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Call (262) 555-0148.
